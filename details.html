About

I received a BA (Honors) Graphic and Communcation Design degree from Central Saint Martins in 2015 and have since worked professionally as a graphic designer and software engineer. My writing practice encompasses life on the internet, theory of disability, and forms of memory; my writing has appeared in The New Inquiry, The Outline, and on my blog. I strive to make my work in all forms clear, informative, and fun.

Interests

Media and internet culture, psychogeography, forms of categorization, maps, memory, critical media studies, disability culture, languages and linguistics, Situationism, cities, diaries and journals, fragrance, typography, psychoanalysis.
